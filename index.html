some of the services offered

private security personnel

While having a bodyguard may be a bit of a status symbol, they can be a necessity to protect executives and VIPs from unexpected incidents and threatening behavior. For VIPs or executives who require the services of private security personnel for themselves, clients and family, UNCO Global security is here to meet all of your security needs. With our qualified private security personnel who are equipped with high tech security gadgets and intelligence, we ensure to intercept potential threats and ensure your security. They are also trained to know what to look and listen for in the immediate surroundings. They act as your chauffeur to bring you to your destination safely.

Corporate security guards

At UNCO Global security, we ensure that your company premises is well protected. We provide corporate security guards who ensure the safety of your members of staff and that of your clients who come into your premises. We ensure proper security and safety measures are in place while controlling access of physical assets within your premises.

Event security personnel

UNCO Global Security is skilled in the area of crowd management and venue security ensuring your event goes on without a hitch. It is common for people to let their guard down during large social gatherings in order to participate in the festivities. UNCO Global Security ensures that we are your eyes when you are not looking. From security screening of individuals before access is granted into the venue to a full security sweep of the venue before and during the event, we ensure your safety during your event.

Security awareness training

Being security conscious cannot be over emphasized! At UNCO Global security, we make it our priority to provide security training and awareness to corporate facilities and security agents. We train them on how to be sensitive for security threats within their environment and what to do in the event of a breach in security.
